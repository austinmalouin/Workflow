- **Tags** (13/13 used): debt free bundle, budget planner set, debt snowball, finance bundle,

## What's weak

1. **Title has 50 unused characters** (90/140) — real keyword real estate being left on the table.
2. **Zero mention of "printable" or "undated"** in the title — both are core buyer-intent terms
   for this niche (confirmed via keyword research below) and neither appears anywhere in the
   current title or tags.
3. **Tags waste slots on near-duplicates of the title** ("debt snowball" tag when title already
   says "Debt Snowball") instead of covering adjacent search terms — Etsy's own guidance is to use
   tags for terms *not* already in the title.
4. **"monthly budget" tag is a mismatch** — the bundle includes a *Weekly* Budget Planner, not a
   monthly one; this tag likely draws clicks that bounce.
5. No tie-in tag to the shop's differentiated ADHD/neurodivergent line even as a soft signal (this
   bundle is the natural upsell path from an ADHD listing — a shopper who lands on the ADHD Debt
   Payoff Kit should be able to find this bundle via overlapping tags).

## Keyword research (WebSearch, 2026-07-22)

- Etsy tag rules confirmed current: 13 tags, ≤20 characters each, no duplicate the title verbatim;
  multi-word (2-4 word) long-tail phrases outperform single words for conversion.
- Title: 140-char hard limit, but only the **first ~40 characters display in mobile search** —
  front-load the primary keyword.
- "Printable," "instant download," "undated" are consistently cited as must-include intent
  modifiers for this exact product category (personal-finance printables).
- Bundle-specific competitor titles that rank well combine "Printable Budget Planner," "Financial
  Planning Bundle," "Debt Free," and "Money Tracker" — validates leaning harder into "printable"
  and "debt free" language than the current title does.

## Recommended new title (119/140 chars)

> **Debt-Free Starter Kit | Printable Budget Planner, Debt Snowball & Savings Tracker Bundle | Undated PDF Instant Download**

Front-loads "Debt-Free Starter Kit" (exact-match differentiator + bundle signal) in the critical
first 40 characters, adds "Printable" and "Undated" (both proven intent terms currently missing),
and works "Instant Download" into the tail instead of a tag slot.
