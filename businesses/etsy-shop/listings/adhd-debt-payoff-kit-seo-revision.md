## What's weak

This listing is already the best-optimized of the 10 (full tag count, decent title) — but it's
still getting zero search traffic, and keyword research surfaced a real gap: the listing never
uses the words **"shame-free"** or **"impulse spending"** (as a phrase) even though those are the
exact emotional-intent phrases neurodivergent buyers search with, and a competing listing
(*"ADHD Money Workbook — A Gentle Finance System for Bills, Impulse Spending, Debt & Anxiety"*)
is using almost exactly that language and ranking. Two tag slots (`debt payoff tracker`,
`debt payoff plan`) are near-duplicates of each other and of the title — wasted real estate.

## Keyword research (WebSearch, 2026-07-22)

- ADHD/neurodivergent productivity planners are one of Etsy's fastest-growing printable
  categories in 2026 (40-200%+ YoY search growth per third-party trend trackers) — this is
  genuinely a good bet to lean harder into, not a niche worth downplaying.
- Competitor listings ranking in this space consistently use "shame-free," "gentle finance," and
  "impulse spending" as literal phrases — "impulse spend log" (current title) is close but not
  the phrase buyers actually type.
- "Neurodivergent" and "ADHD friendly" both confirmed as active, searched tags (already in use
  here — good).

## Recommended new title (107/140 chars)

> **ADHD Debt Payoff Kit | Shame-Free Debt Thermometer & Impulse Spend Log PDF | Neurodivergent Finance Tracker**

Swaps "Visual" for "Shame-Free" — same front-loaded structure, but replaces a purely descriptive
word with the emotional-intent phrase the research shows buyers actually search and competing
(ranking) listings actually use.
